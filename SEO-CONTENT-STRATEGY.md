## 5. FAQ Section (SEO-Optimized)

### Where should FAQs be placed?
- Homepage: Below the fold, after trust signals
- Services page: At bottom before CTA
- City pages: After service description

### Optimized FAQs for Homepage

**Q: How much does porta potty rental cost?**
A: Porta potty rental pricing varies based on several factors: the type of unit (standard, deluxe, or luxury), how many units you need, how long you need them, and your location. Standard units for construction sites typically start around $100-150 per month, while deluxe and luxury units cost more. Call us at (888) 555-0199 for a personalized quote.

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery for portable toilet rental?**
A: Yes! We offer same-day delivery for porta potty rental in most service areas when you call before 2 PM. Delivery times vary by location and availability. For guaranteed same-day delivery, we recommend calling as early as possible. For events, we recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance.

**Q: How many porta potties do I need for my event?**
A: The general rule for event portable toilet rental is: 1 unit per 50 guests for a 4-hour event, or 1 unit per 25 guests for an 8-hour event. If alcohol is being served, add 20% more units. For construction sites, OSHA requires 1 unit per 20 workers. We can help you calculate the right number — just call us with your event details.

**Q: What is included in the rental price?**
A: Our portable toilet rental includes delivery to your location, setup, and for weekly and monthly rentals, regular servicing (cleaning, pumping, sanitizing, and restocking toilet paper and hand sanitizer). The price we quote is the price you pay — no hidden fees.

**Q: Do you provide ADA accessible portable restrooms?**
A: Yes, we offer fully ADA-compliant portable restroom rental units. These include extra-wide doors for wheelchair access, interior grab bars, non-slip flooring, and spacious interiors. ADA units are required for many public events and recommended for any gathering that may include attendees with mobility needs.

**Q: How often are portable toilets serviced?**
A: For weekly and monthly rentals, we provide once-per-week servicing that includes pumping, cleaning, sanitizing, and restocking. For events or high-traffic situations, we offer twice-weekly or even daily servicing. Event rentals are serviced before delivery and picked up after the event.
